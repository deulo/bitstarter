Hours Market is an application where you can exchange services or objects all around the world with no need of money. Users can contribute with people and receive "Hours" to use them as a kind of money to obtain things they need. This system can be used locally or worldwide, depending on the task to be done. Our goal is to make a great community of people helping each other doing things they are good at. We will begin creating a responsive web application (for desktops and mobile) and later we will make the native iOS and Android application.

There are lots of situations when this application has utility. Imagine that you need to cut the grass for your tomorrow party in your garden and you don't have time because you are busy preparing the food. In this case, you can spend one "Hour" of your account to pay someone for cutting the grass. As opposite, If I am good at gardening and I am lazy in the sofa, I can't win one "Hour" of everything I can imagine cutting your grass. That seems a good deal. The objective is to do only what you like to do (cooking, driving a car,...) and paying "Hours" for what you hate to do (ironing, writing an article,...)
